Module Guess.Next_best_guesses

type t =
  1. | Not_computed
  2. | Computed of T.t list

When computed, best guesses are sorted by decreasing number of expected_bits_gained. The functions in this module do not compute best guesses recursively, thus they will all produce guesses where next_best_guesses = Not_computed. For computed guesses, see the opening book.
